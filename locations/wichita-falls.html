Wichita Falls Resort

An inviting boutique destination for pets in north Texas. Enjoy boarding, daycare, grooming and more.

Welcome to Wichita Falls

Nestled just south of town, our Wichita Falls resort delivers the same high‑end experience our Amarillo guests love. Spacious indoor/outdoor suites, attentive caregivers and curated play sessions ensure every pet’s stay is filled with comfort and joy. Our facility is designed for peace of mind—climate control, secure yards and round‑the‑clock monitoring make it a true home away from home.

We’re proud to serve the greater Wichita Falls community with luxury boarding, playful daycare, spa‑quality grooming and a charming boutique stocked with pet essentials and indulgences.
