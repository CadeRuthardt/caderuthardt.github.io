Amarillo Resort

Premium boarding, daycare, grooming and training in the heart of Amarillo.

Welcome to Amarillo

Set on expansive grounds with seven outdoor yards and two indoor play arenas, our Amarillo location offers a boutique retreat for discerning pets. Climate‑controlled suites, soothing music and comfortable bedding create an oasis of calm, while our highly trained team provides attentive care and enrichment. Whether your companion is here for a day of structured play, an overnight stay, a spa treatment or obedience training, they'll enjoy a safe, luxurious experience.

We also host an upscale pet boutique stocked with toys, treats and accessories, so you can take a piece of the resort experience home.
