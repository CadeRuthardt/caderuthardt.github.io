Luxury Boarding

Resort‑style accommodations for dogs and cats. Because your pet deserves the best.

Accommodations

Our boarding suites are designed to feel like a five‑star hotel. Dogs can choose from standard suites or spacious villas, each furnished with cosy bedding, natural light and climate control. Cats lounge in private condos stocked with soft blankets and climbing perches.

Every stay includes daily housekeeping, fresh water and premium meals. We offer webcam access in select suites so you can check in on your pet from anywhere.
