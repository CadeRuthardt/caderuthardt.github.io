Our Story

Passionately caring for pets like family.

How It Began

Le Chateau Pet Resort was founded with a simple mission: provide a resort‑style experience where pets are as pampered as their owners. We set out to create a safe haven that blends luxury, comfort and fun for our four‑legged guests.

Our first location opened in Amarillo and quickly became the area’s premier destination for boarding, daycare and grooming. We expanded to Wichita Falls and continue to grow while staying true to our core values of safety, cleanliness and personalized attention.

Mission & Values

  • Safety: We adhere to the highest safety standards and maintain round‑the‑clock monitoring.
  • Comfort: Luxurious suites, climate control and soothing music create a restful retreat.
  • Expertise: Our team includes certified trainers and professional groomers.
  • Personalization: We tailor experiences to each pet’s needs and temperament.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated staff love animals as much as you do. From our front‑desk concierges to our caretakers and groomers, each team member is trained in animal behaviour, health and first aid. We pride ourselves on providing gentle, attentive care.
